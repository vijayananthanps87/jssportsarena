Booking Policies

Advance Booking

Book online and pay before you play. Extra time can be booked via the online system.

Cancellation

Cancellations must be made via the booking system at least 24 hours before your booking to be eligible for JS Sports Arena booking credits.

Payment

Payment is required at the time of booking. On-site payments are accepted by EFTPOS only.
